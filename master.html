MASTER Program

Advanced Studies in Aerospace

The MASTER program offers advanced education in aerospace engineering, focusing on specialized areas and research opportunities. Students can choose from various tracks to align their education with their career goals.

TAS ASTRO

TECHNICAL AND SPACE STUDIES

The advanced master "Space Systems Engineering" provides high-level interdisciplinary training in space systems engineering and space project management. The program allows students to acquire and develop technical skills specific to space system design and to understand the international, economic, and legal aspects of space programs.

The advanced master "Space Systems Engineering" leads to systems or management positions in various space applications programs (Earth Observation, Telecommunications, Navigations, Science, Human Spaceflight...) as researchers and design engineers in space industry, agencies, or laboratories.

TAS ASTRO SEEDS

Space Exploration Development Systems


The SEEDS International Placement


The SEEDS (Space Exploration Development Systems) international placement is an optional extra project during which you will work in multidisciplinary teams on topical space exploration research projects, designed in collaboration with advisors from the space industry.


ISAE-SUPAERO's partners in SEEDS are Politecnico di Torino in Italy and the University of Leicester in the UK. All three institutions have strong links with the space industry, a heritage of space research and exploration, and high-level expertise in the delivery of teaching. You will spend two months at each institution and will be required to cover basic travel and subsistence costs. The course is taught in English at all three sites.


The international SEEDS program is supported and endorsed by the Italian (ASI), French (CNES), and UK Space Agencies, as well as Thales Alenia Space, ALTEC, and numerous other companies and institutions.
