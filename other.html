Other Programs

Specialized Educational Opportunities

Explore a variety of specialized educational programs designed to enhance knowledge and skills in various fields of aerospace and engineering.

DAST

Diploma in Aerospace and Space Technology

The DAST program provides in-depth knowledge in aerospace and space technology, preparing students for careers in these dynamic fields.

ENSAM

École Nationale Supérieure d'Arts et Métiers

This collaboration with ENSAM offers students the opportunity to engage in advanced engineering projects and coursework.

Saoudiens

Saudi Arabian Program

The Saudi Arabian program focuses on enhancing the skills and knowledge of students from Saudi Arabia in the field of aerospace.

X Executive Master

Executive Master Program

The X Executive Master program is designed for professionals seeking to advance their careers with in-depth knowledge and skills in aerospace management and technology.

EAE

European Aerospace Engineering

The EAE program offers a comprehensive education in aerospace engineering with a European perspective, preparing students for international careers.

H2

Hydrogen Technology Program

The H2 program focuses on hydrogen technology, providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to innovate in this emerging field.

MOOC

Massive Open Online Courses

Our MOOCs offer flexible and accessible learning opportunities in various aerospace and engineering topics, allowing students to learn at their own pace.
